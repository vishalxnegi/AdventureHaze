Popular Places

Shimla
Shimla is a mesmerizig hill station in Himachal Pradesh. Often called as the 'Queen of Hills', Shimla is the capital city of Himachal Pradesh and is one of the popular Himachal Pradesh tourism destinations. Shimla is also one of the top tourist places in India
Manali
Manali is a breathtakingly beautiful hill station nestled in the mountains of Himachal Pradesh near the northern end of the Kullu Valley. Manali is renowned for its snow-covered mountains, scenic beauty, history and culture.Often referred to as the 'Valley of the Gods'.
Dalhousie
Dalhousie is a scenic hill station in Chamba district of Himachal Pradesh.The beautiful Dalhousie is spread around five hills, known as Katalagh, Potreyn, Bakrota, Terah and Bhangora. Victorian architecture are prevalent in the bungalows and churches in the town.
